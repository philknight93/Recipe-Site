## Ingredients
- 2 cans tuna
- 1 can peas
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 pkg noodles ???
- salt
- pepper
- milk

## Instructions
1. Cook noodles and drain
2. Butter 13x9 dish
3. Layer tuna, peas, soup, noodles
4. Pour milk around the edges (???)
5. Bake at 350F for 35-45 mins
