## Ingredients
- 3 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup oats
- 1 1/2 tsp pumpkin spice
- 3 eggs
- 16 oz pumpkin
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1/2 cup evaporated milk
- 1 cup nuts, chopped

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients together and put in 2 9x5 loaf pans
