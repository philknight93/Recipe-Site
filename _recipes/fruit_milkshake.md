## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup banana or 1/2 cup fresh strawberries
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup water
- 4 pks Equal
- 3 ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Put all ingredients in blender
2. Blend on high until ice is completely blended (~3 mins)
