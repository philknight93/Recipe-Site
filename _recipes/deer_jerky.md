## Ingredients
- 8 Tbs soy sauce
- 8 Tbs worcestershire sauce
- 2 Tbs catsup
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp onion salt
- few drops liquid smoke
- 1/2 tsp cajun spice
- Deer meat

## Instructions
1. Marinate meat in mixture 45 mins
2. Prepare jerky
