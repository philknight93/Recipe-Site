---
title: Cheese Dip
description: velveeta...yum
tags: family snack
source: Alice Belew
yield: 
ingredients:
- 1 lb Velveeta cheese
- 1/2 lb sausage
- 1/2 lb ground beef
- 1 sm. onion, chopped
- 1 sm. bell pepper, chopped
- 1 tomato, diced
- 6 Tbs jalapeno relish
instructions: 
- Brown sausage, hamburger and onions.
- Melt cheese in crock pot
- Mix in meat and all otehr ingredients
- Let warm
