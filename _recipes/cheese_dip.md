## Ingredients
- 1 lb Velveeta cheese
- 1/2 lb sausage
- 1/2 lb ground beef
- 1 sm. onion, chopped
- 1 sm. bell pepper, chopped
- 1 tomato, diced
- 6 Tbs jalapeno relish

## Instructions
1. Brown sausage, hamburger and onions.
2. Melt cheese in crock pot
3. Mix in meat and all otehr ingredients
4. Let warm
