## Ingredients
- 2/3 cup shortening
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup water
- 3 eggs

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350F
2. Cream shortening and sugar. Add in vanilla while creaming
3. Add flour and water slowly into the mixture
4. Add eggs slowly into the mixture
5. Bake for 30 minutes at 350F
