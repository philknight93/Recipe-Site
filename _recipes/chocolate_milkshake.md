## Ingredients
- 1 pk sugar free hot cocoa mix
- 1/2 cup mik
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 pks equal / sweet & low
- 3 ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Put all ingredients in blender
2. Blend on high until ice is completely blended (~3 mins)
