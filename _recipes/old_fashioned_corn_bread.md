---
title: Old Fashioned Corn Bread
description: yum
tags: family side
source: Marie Probus
yield: 9x9 pan
ingredients:
- 1 cup yellow corn meal (Martha White S.R. Buttermilk corn meal)
- 1 cup flour
- 2 Tbs sugar
- 1/3 cup shortening ( or butter )
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
instructions: 
- Mix ingredient together
- Melt butter in skillet and fry
- OR bake 25 mins @ 350F
