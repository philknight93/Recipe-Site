## Ingredients
- 1 cup yellow corn meal (Martha White S.R. Buttermilk corn meal)
- 1 cup flour
- 2 Tbs sugar
- 1/3 cup shortening ( or butter )
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk

## Instructions
1. Mix ingredient together
2. Melt butter in skillet and fry
3. OR bake 25 mins @ 350F
