## Ingredients
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 small can condensed milk
- 1 cup peanut butter

## Instructions
1. Bring sugar and milk to boil, stirring constantly
2. Cook to a soft boil and stir for 5 mins
3. Cool slightly
4. Add peanut butter and beat until creamy
5. Pour into buttered pan
