---
title: Almond Fudge
description: ''
tags: family dessert draft
source: Barbara Rearden
yield:
ingredients:
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 (tsp or Tbs?) whiye syrup
- 3 Tbs butter
- 1/2 tsp almond extract
- 1/2 cup toasted almonds, coarsely chopped
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
instructions:
- In 3 qt pan combine sugar, milk, syrup, and salt
- Cook and stir over medium heat until sugar is dissolved
- Cook (without stirring) until soft boil (~256F)
- Remove from heat and add butter (do not stir)
- Cook without stirring to 110F
- Add vanilla and almond extract
- Stir vigorously until fidge stiffens
